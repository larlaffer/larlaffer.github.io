Concert Preview

Spring Concert

Sunday June 1, 2025, 3:00 PM
Anderson Community Theatre

The Anderson Community Band (ACB) presents the 2024-25 Concert Series Finale at Anderson Center Theatre on June 1, at 3:00 P.M. The concert will feature the music of contemporary and classic band composers. The ACB will perform The Falls, a descriptive work portraying the splendor of Niagara Falls by Rossano Galante and a tribute to The Women of the British Isles, composed by ACB founder, David Martin.

Featured soloists include ACB trumpet trio Brad Benick, Alex Siebert and Justin Moeller performing Trumpets Wild by Harold Walters. Richard Rogers's Waltz from Cinderella and Tchaikovsky's epic Marche Slav will highlight a concert that has something for everyone.

The concert is free and will last approximately one hour. We hope to see you there.
